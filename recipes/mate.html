Mate

What is Mate?

Mate is a drink from Argentina and It's a national drink. It can be amargo or soft with sugar

How make it

  1. Get the materials: Termo, mate, bombilla and yerba.
  2. Boil water between 75ºC and 85ºC and pour it into the termo.
  3. Throw 3/4 of yerba into the mate.
  4. Shake the mate with your hand while you cover the top and try to make a "mountain".
  5. Pour water over half without yerba up to half.
  6. Wait a few minutes and put the bombilla into mate.
  7. Enjoy your first Mate!
