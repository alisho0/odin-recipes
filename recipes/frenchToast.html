French Toast

What is French Toast?

French toast is a dish made by soaking slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk, then frying them until golden brown. It's often flavored with vanilla and cinnamon and served with toppings like maple syrup or fresh fruit.

How make it

  1. Prepare the Egg Mixture
  2. Soak the Bread
  3. Cook the French Toast
  4. Serve Warm
  5. Optional Toppings
